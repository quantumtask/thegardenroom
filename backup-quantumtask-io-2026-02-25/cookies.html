What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files on your device that remember preferences or help us understand site use. Similar technologies include local storage and pixels.

How we use cookies

We may use lightweight analytics to understand performance and interest. If used, they stay basic and privacy-respecting. We do not intentionally place ad retargeting pixels.

Types of cookies

  • Necessary: Enable core site security and navigation.
  • Analytics: Measure page performance and traffic patterns (if enabled).
  • Third-party embeds: Links to WhatsApp or social profiles may allow those services to set their own cookies when used.

Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies in your browser. The site should still work, but some preferences or embeds may not.
